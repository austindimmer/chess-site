FUNDAMENTAL RULES

  • White ALWAYS moves first
  • If you intentionally touch one of your own pieces you must move it
  • Castling is a special safety maneuver that allows the King to move two squares toward a Rook and the Rook to hop over it, but only if neither piece has moved before and the King is not in or passing through check.
  • If a pawn successfully reaches the furthest opposite side of the board, it must instantly transform into a Queen, Rook, Bishop, or Knight of the same color.
  • You win by checkmate when the opponent's King is under attack and cannot escape, but it is a stalemate if their King is not in check and they have no legal moves left on the board.
